Air Amplifiers
Key Features
  • Construction shape optimised to maximise the Coanda effect
  • Possibility to regulate the flow with an adjustable ring
  • Wide vacuum and blow-off section, suitable for a variety of applications
  • Instant operation
  • No moving parts, so not subject to wear
  • No electricity or chemical products required
  • More efficient than Venturi devices and enjectors
  • No sparks or interferences
  • Reliable and maintenance-free
Description

The AM Series round air-flow amplifiers offer excellent performance for suction and blow-off. The quality of the design and construction optimise the Coanda effect so they use a small amount of compressed air to generate a powerful, high-speed flow. They perform a dual function of vacuum and blow-off: for ventilating electrical panels, conveying fumes and lightweight particles produced by machining, conveying and handling of light parts, drying and cooling. When combined with the VR Series coolers, they create an effective patented system where, by conveying the flow of hot air discharged by the cooler and feeding it into the AM Series amplifier, the cooling power is optimised, so making it possible to drawn hot air out of enclosures or ventilating closed areas to be cooled. The air flow rate can be adjusted by simply turning the nut at the inlet.
