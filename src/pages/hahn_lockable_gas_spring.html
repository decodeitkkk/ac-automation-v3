Functional principle of our Lockable Gas spring

The type of locking mechanism of our HAHN gas spring can be calculated and tailored to your individual requirements during production . When the release is activated, the piston valve of the gas spring opens. This causes nitrogen to flow through the piston in the spring-lockable gas spring and oil to flow through the piston in the rigid-lockable gas spring. The incoming gas or oil extends the piston rod or allows it to be pushed in. When the release is released again, the valve in the gas spring closes automatically and the piston rod is locked in the desired position.

Areas of application

Our lockable gas springs can be locked steplessly over the entire stroke. Flaps, furniture doors or other moving parts can thus be fixed in a desired position and then moved again. Examples: pieces of furniture, vehicle seats, flaps in boat building HAHN release systems are specially designed and adapted for your applications and requirements. For the lockable gas springs, we offer lever releases, Bowden cable releases and hydraulic releases. With our wide range of release systems , you will always find the best and most convenient solution for you.
