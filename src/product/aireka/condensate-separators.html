Description

The main strengths of the condensate separators Series HSC are effectiveness, reliability, and versatility.

  • Effectiveness: The removal of condensate is achieved through the unique design of the DryVolution system. This system features a series of concentric flanges, assembled with a precise angle of incidence to the direction of the incoming flow. This setup generates a compressed air expansion within a chamber downstream of the flanges, leading to a significant decrease in temperature and, consequently, condensation of humidity. The condensed moisture is directed to the bottom of the bowl.
  • Reliability: No electric power or chemical substances are used, and there are no moving parts apart from the automatic drain. This ensures steady performance and virtually zero maintenance.
  • Versatility: The range covers a wide spectrum of flow-rates and technical features. The materials used and the assembly make it a very sturdy product, suitable for many different applications: upstream of coalescing filters (cleaning of air inside clean rooms), downstream of large compressors for air distribution in factories, on board trucks and agricultural machines, upstream of pneumatic tools, etc.
