Skipchek Kinecheks

SLIMLINE and SUPER-K KINECHEKS can be provided with a SKIPCHEK feature in ½ inch, 1 inch, 2 inch, 3 inch, 4 inch, and 6 inch stroke models. The fast feed (skip) interval reduces machining times when it is desired to line drill tubular sections, clevis parts etc. (tube drilling). The skip feature is accomplished by adding a “by-pass” flow passage to the cylinder, thus permitting the SKIPCHEK to advance with minimal resistance over the length of the skip. The skip is custom engineered to each user’s specifications. Since Deschner SKIPCHEKS avoid the complexity of added moving parts and seals, the user will benefit by receiving the reliable performance and long service life provided by all Kinecheks.
